📌 Project Description  
The Street Vendor Digitalization Agent is a multi-agent, cloud-native conversational AI designed to empower informal micro-entrepreneurs by bridging the digital and financial divide. Built using IBM watsonx Orchestrate and powered by IBM Granite foundation models, the system acts as a 24/7 digital consultant that provides accessible, step-by-step guidance on critical business operations.  
To ensure factual accuracy and reliability, the solution utilizes a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline to translate complex government micro-credit policies—specifically the PM SVANidhi scheme—into easy-to-understand, actionable checklists. Beyond financial assistance, specialized agents within the system actively guide vendors through fintech onboarding and hyperlocal search optimization.  
⚠️ The Problem  
Street vendors and informal micro-entrepreneurs often lack access to formal financial systems, digital payment infrastructure, and modern local search visibility. Bureaucratic hurdles, confusing jargon in government schemes (like PM SVANidhi), and technical illiteracy prevent them from modernizing their businesses, leaving them vulnerable to economic instability.  
💡 Proposed Solution  
Accessible, Zero-Friction Interface: A mobile-friendly, web-based conversational assistant that requires no app downloads, no logins, and zero prior technical expertise to operate.  
Government Scheme Simplification (PM SVANidhi): Translates complex, bureaucratic micro-credit policies into simple, conversational, and actionable step-by-step checklists.  
Fintech & Payment Enablement: Provides jargon-free guidance to help hawkers independently set up merchant UPI accounts, complete e-KYC, and generate QR code standees.  
Hyperlocal SEO & Visibility: Actively guides vendors on how to drop precise geolocation pins on digital maps to ensure their physical carts are discoverable by nearby customers.  
Empathetic, Jargon-Free AI: Powered by tailored behavioral guardrails that strip away confusing corporate terminology, replacing it with an encouraging, peer-to-peer conversational tone.  
🛠️ Technology Used  
IBM watsonx Orchestrate: For designing and orchestrating multi-agent workflows visually without complex coding.  
IBM Granite Models: (e.g., ibm-granite-3-2-8b) For natural language understanding, reasoning, and providing accessible, personalized guidance.  
IBM Cloud: Provides a reliable, secure, and scalable infrastructure to build and deploy the Agent.  
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG): Fetches real policy data first, then generates intelligent answers, making the Agent trustworthy and factually accurate regarding government schemes.  
IBM watsonx.ai: For model deployment, embeddings, and AI governance.  
Vector Database (FAISS/Chroma): For RAG-based retrieval of unstructured financial guidelines, KYC requirements, and municipal policy documents.
